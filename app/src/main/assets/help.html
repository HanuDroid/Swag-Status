Getting Started

Thankyou for downloading this app.
This application is very user friendly and does not need any help.

However, for your reference, we have documented some information that you may need.

Navigation:

Just swipe to right / left on the screen to navigate to next / previous Swag Status.
Switch to landscape layout to access a list of all Swag Statuses at a time.

Notifications about new Swag Status:

We updates Swag Status on daily basis. Whenever a new Swag Status is posted, we will notify.

Submit Statuses:

Share your Swag Status with your friends by submitting a Swag Status in the app. Find the menu option to upload a new Swag Status. The Swag Status will be reviewed by the moderators before they are published.
Once the Swag Status is approved / rejected, the user will be notified.

Memes:

You can get Memes (Graphical images) also. The images are stored in internal storage space.
Memes older than 7 days are automatically deleted to optimize storage space.
If you do not wish to get memes, you can deactivate them in settings screen.

Report bugs

To request a new feature or to report a bug in the app, please write Email to the developer.
